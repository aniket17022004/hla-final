Hindi Literary Association (HLA) working tirelessly towards the growth, progress and promotion of Hindi Language, the mother tongue of the majority of Indian population.

Club promotes Hindi Literature and provides a platform to Hindi speaking students to showcase their literary skills. To help other students who are interested in Hindi Literature and History gain an insight into the same.

We conduct various events with Hindi being the medium to prove to be an epitome of creativity. There is hardly any area where we haven't left a mark. When it comes to social awareness, we embrace it with full responsibility.

HLA organizes various programmes every year, to enhance thoughts and speech through groups and sessions, to conduct events that provide insight into India's History and Heritage. It has a motto to revist the old scriptures of Hindi Literature and make people aware of how diverse our Cultural Heritage is.
